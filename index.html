Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers from Wade about ponds, fountains, and garden design.

How much does a custom luxury fish pond cost in Cambodia?

Costs typically range from $15,000 to $50,000+ depending on the size, materials, and complexity of the filtration system. We provide bespoke quotes tailored to your specific space and vision.

How long does a custom pond and garden installation take?

Most luxury projects are completed within 2 to 4 weeks. This timeline includes excavation, premium liner and multi-stage filter installation, rock work, and water cycling.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance for ponds and gardens?

Yes, The Garden Guy offers comprehensive seasonal maintenance packages, including pond cleaning, filter servicing, water quality testing, and landscape pruning.

How often should a koi pond be cleaned in Cambodia's tropical climate?

In Cambodia's tropical climate, we recommend a basic cleaning and filter check every 2-4 weeks, with a deep clean and water quality assessment every 3-6 months.

How do you manage water quality during the Cambodian rainy season?

Heavy downpours can alter pond pH and introduce debris. We install robust overflow systems and premium filtration, and recommend regular water testing during the monsoon season.

What are the best plants for a luxury garden in Phnom Penh?

We recommend a mix of tropical evergreens, architectural palms, and flowering plants like Frangipani and Bougainvillea, which thrive in the local climate.
